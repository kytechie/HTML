Tasty Banofee Pie

Banoffee pie, is a dessert combining banana, toffee (or caramel), and whipped cream, often with
a biscuit or pastry base. The name is a portmanteau of "banana" and "toffee".
It was created in the 1970s in England and has become a popular dessert, with various recipes and variations.

Recipe Information

Prep Time:45 minutes

Cook Time:4 hours

Serving:6

Difficulty:Beginner friendly

Ingredients

  • Biscuit base - Digestives, Mcvites or Crackers
  • 5 fingers of Bananas
  • 2 litres Boiled Condensed Milk
  • 1 Whipped cream
  • 4 kg Chocolate Shavings
  • 1/2 tea soon of Salt
    •

Follow through to make the best Banofee

  1. Melt the butter and mix well together with crushed digestives.
  2. Press the biscuit mixture firmly into a flan tin with the back of a spoon and chill.
  3. Gently melt the butter and sugar, until the sugar is dissolved. Add the condensed milk
  4. Pour this onto your chilled base, return to the fridge. When set, add the bananas.
  5. Spoon the whipped cream on top and spread evenly.
  6. Finish off with chocolate shavings.
  7. Freeze in the fridge for 1:30 minutes
